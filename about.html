Our Story

A legacy of conservation, education, and wildlife protection in Singapore

Our Mission

To create compelling experiences that connect people and wildlife, inspiring efforts to care for our natural world.

Our Vision

To be a world-leading zoological institution that inspires people to value and conserve biodiversity.

A Historic Union

In 2024, Singapore Zoo and Jurong Bird Park joined forces to create the Singapore Wildlife Foundation, marking a new chapter in wildlife conservation and education in Southeast Asia.

  • Enhanced visitor experience
  • Expanded conservation efforts
  • Integrated research facilities
  • Combined expertise and resources
